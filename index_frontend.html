Cekadak

Maksud perkataan

Bigmouthed; fussy.

Contoh Ayat

1. Banyak cekadaklah kau ni! Nak ni, nak tu... Diam boleh tak?

3. I don't like working with him. Too fussy.

Beruk Mak Yeh
  • Songeh
  • Perangai
Maksud perkataan

Hooligans. From Malay. Beruk = Ape; Mak Yeh = No meaning. Not used in Malaysian English.

Contoh Ayat

1. Perangai macam beruk Mak Yeh.

2.Acting like hooligans.

Capub
  • Capab
  • Capap
Maksud perkataan

Seeking publicity. From Malay: Cari publisiti. Cari = Seek; Publisiti = Publicity. Generally has the same meaning as cagla, though capub is more commonly used. Usually used as mencapub. Not used in Malaysian English.

Contoh Ayat

1. Kuat mencapub si Shira tu.

2.Shira likes to seek publicity.
