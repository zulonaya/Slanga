Bantu Kami Menilai Perkataan Baharu

Adakah perkataan ini sesuai untuk kamus Slanga?

Maksud perkataan

Seeking publicity. From Malay: Cari publisiti. Cari = Seek; Publisiti = Publicity. Generally has the same meaning as cagla, though capub is more commonly used. Usually used as mencapub.

Contoh Ayat
Kuat mencapub si Shira tu. / Shira likes to seek publicity.
